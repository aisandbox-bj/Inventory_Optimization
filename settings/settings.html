03 · Operational context

Short, generic site-character text injected as {customerContext} at the top of every LLM prompt. Pick from the library — entries are deliberately generic, no client identifiers. The Custom slot is capped at 300 chars and lint-checked for things like long numeric IDs, company suffixes, all-caps codes, emails, or IPs.

06 · Multi-plant analysis

When ON, future Intake sessions will offer a scope-picker to choose which plants to include when files carry data for more than one plant; reads through to plant-conditional column selection (e.g. openPO's GM PO Qty vs MLA PO Qty). When OFF (the default), assessments are treated as single-plant — same behaviour as today. Note: the scope-picker modal and the plant-conditional column logic are queued for follow-up chunks (T-01e and T-01f); this toggle currently only stores your preference, no analytical effect yet.
